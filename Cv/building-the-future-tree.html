How to read this

Each square in the charts below represents about 100 hours of meaningful exposure: building, shipping, auditing, forecasting, mentoring, governing, or operating in that domain.

Hour model

  • Role duration weighted by the share of work that clearly sat in each domain.
  • Adjusted upward when the repo contains strong delivery proof, repeated practice, or measured outcomes.
  • Adjusted downward when claims are adjacent but not deeply evidenced.
  • Kept intentionally conservative so the story stays defensible.

Narrative frame

  • The roots are not old work left behind. They still feed the way you think.
  • The trunk is the present operating level you genuinely own today.
  • The branches show where the next step naturally grows, without inflating titles.
Assumption note: these hour estimates are based on the evidence already in the repo, especially the capability tree, role history, and named delivery outcomes. They are meant to support storytelling, visual comparison, and future MCP-style capability modelling. They should be treated as explainable estimates, not audited labour records.
