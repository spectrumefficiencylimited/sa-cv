## Writing Rule

Every bullet should pass this test:

> Could I explain this in 30 seconds with a real example?

If not, rewrite it.

## Bullet Formula

Use:

`Action + system + outcome`

Examples:

- Designed enterprise data warehouse models supporting student lifecycle analytics.
- Implemented Type 2 warehouse patterns to track longitudinal changes across agencies.
- Built automated ETL pipelines in R and SQL, improving delivery reliability and reporting accuracy.
- Led Microsoft Purview implementation to strengthen stewardship, classification, and information risk visibility.

## Guardrails

- Keep the story human and chronological.
- Shift emphasis, not facts.
- Use neutral verbs: led, designed, implemented, built, established, partnered.
- Use numbers only when you can defend them.
- Avoid stacked adjectives and inflated claims.
