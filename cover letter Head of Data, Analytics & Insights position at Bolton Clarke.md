About Us

Bolton Clarke is Australia’s largest independent, not‑for‑profit aged care provider shaping the future of positive ageing. With RSL Queensland and the Royal District Nursing Service at the heart of our DNA, we’ve cared for Australians since 1885. Today, our teams support more than 130,000 people to live independently at home and across our retirement living communities and residential aged care homes. Everything we do is dedicated to enabling, celebrating and supporting older Australians to age positively.
