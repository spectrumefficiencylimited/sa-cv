# Bolton Clarke - Head of Data, Analytics and Insights

## Role Summary

Bolton Clarke is seeking an experienced Head of Data, Analytics and Insights to lead its enterprise data and analytics capability and enable evidence-based decision-making across the organisation.

This is a senior leadership role responsible for translating business strategy into trusted insights, modern data platforms, and scalable analytics that directly improve service delivery, performance, and customer outcomes.

## About the Role

- Lead enterprise analytics, reporting, and insight delivery from descriptive through to predictive
- Embed advanced analytics, AI, and automation into business processes
- Partner with executives to define analytics priorities and measurable outcomes
- Oversee data management, governance, quality, privacy, and ethical use
- Lead modern data and analytics platforms and advise on emerging technologies
- Build and develop high-performing analytics, data, and BI teams
- Champion data literacy and analytics maturity across the organisation

## About the Candidate

- Senior experience leading enterprise analytics and or data functions
- Strong stakeholder engagement and executive partnering capability
- Proven delivery of data-driven outcomes in complex organisations
- Deep understanding of data governance, privacy, and regulatory environments
- Experience leading multidisciplinary technical teams
- Exposure to advanced analytics, AI or ML, and modern data platforms is highly desirable
- Tertiary qualifications in data, analytics, information systems, or related fields are required

## Organisation Context

Bolton Clarke is Australia's largest independent not-for-profit aged care provider. The organisation supports more than 130,000 people through care at home, retirement living, and residential aged care services.
